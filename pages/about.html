The Studio

About Deglane Interiors

Deglane Interiors is a boutique interior design practice dedicated to creating spaces that feel authentically yours. We believe that a home should be a sanctuary—a place that restores and inspires you.

Founded on the principles of minimalism and warmth, our approach combines clean architectural lines with organic textures and soft, neutral palettes. We don't just design rooms; we curate atmospheres that stand the test of time.

Our Philosophy

We believe in "Warm Minimalism." It's not about having less for the sake of less, but about making room for what truly matters. By stripping away the unnecessary, we highlight the beauty of natural light, honest materials, and thoughtful craftsmanship.

Every project begins with a conversation. We listen deeply to understand how you live, what you love, and how you want to feel in your space.
