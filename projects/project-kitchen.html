The Minimalist Kitchen

Location: New York, NY Year: 2024 Size: 450 sq ft

This kitchen renovation focused on creating a calm, clutter-free environment using a palette of warm beiges and natural stone. The goal was to maximize natural light and create a seamless flow between the cooking and dining areas.

We utilized custom cabinetry with hidden handles to maintain clean lines, paired with a honed marble countertop that adds texture and elegance. The lighting design features warm LED strips and statement pendant lights to create a cozy atmosphere in the evenings.
